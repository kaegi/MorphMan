Coup d'etat Arc Ending Speech

Link to subtitled version on youtube: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wH2UEymn3Hs

This text file automatically appears when you reach the ending of the coup d'etat arc where the speech is given. Simply read the following lines as you would the rest of MLA, using the romaji over each one to ensure you're at the right place.

(Waga shin'ai naru nippon kokumin no mina-sama)
yYuuhizuTo all the people of my beloved Japan...v

(Nagaku ni watari tadai na kunan wo shiiteiru koto, makoto ni moshiwake naku omoimasu)
yYuuhizuI sincerely apologize for forcing such lengthy and demanding trials upon all of you.v

(Kotabi no jiken wa, wakaku inochi ga kuni no ayamachi, shiite wa watakushi no itaranasa wo tadasou ga shita yue ni kekki de arimashita)
yYuuhizuThis incident, this rebellion, took place because young lives sought to correct the faults of the state, my own imperfections.v

(Irma-shou'i......itsuka kanarazu......anata no kokyou e...)
yTamazu2nd Lt. Irma......someday, I will......visit your homeland......v

(Ka no mono tachi no shogyou wa kesshite yurusareru mono de wa arimasen)
yYuuhizuTheir deeds are surely not ones which may be forgiven.v

(Saredo, nippon no mezame wo negai, yamu ni yamarezu tachiagatta sono kokorozashi made wo karonzuru koto wa dekimasen)
yYuuhizuHowever, I cannot look down upon their wish to see Japan awaken, their wills which compelled them to stand up and fight.v

(Watakushi tachi no kokoro ni kizamitsuke, kaerimiru beki wa nipponjin toshite arubeki makoto no sugata ni aru to omoi itarimashita)
yYuuhizuI have come to believe that we should instead reflect upon the people, the true Japanese people we should strive to be, and carve that ideal into our hearts.v

(Sore ga hiite wa, jinrui ga ichigan to nari, kyoudai na teki ni tachimukau tame no chikara to naru desho)
yYuuhizuThat will someday become the strength that allows humanity to come together as one and face our formidable adversaries.v

(Nagaku ni wataru senran no owariba wa imada miezu, mina-sama no kokoro ni wa fuan no ookina uneri to natte oshiyoseteiru koto desho)
yYuuhizuThe ending of this prolonged war remains out of reach, a fact which surely brings great anxiety to all of you.v

(Kore de......kaereru basho wa naku natta)
yChizuruzuNow......I have nowhere to go home to.v

(Dakara koso watakushi tachi wa, ima to iu jidai wo kyoujin na seishin wo motte ayumaneba narimasen)
yYuuhizuAnd that is why we must take this present age in stride and with a tenacious mind.v

(Teki wo kudaku tame no kiba wo harakara ni mukezaru wo enakatta karera wa, mi wo motte sore wo shimesou to shita no desu)
yYuuhizuThose who were forced to turn their blades against friend rather than foe were trying to show us that through their own actions.v

(Watakushi tachi no kokoro mo, ima futatabi hokori to chikara wo yobimodosu tame ni)
yYuuhizuIn order to bring strength and pride back into our own hearts.v

(Zashite erareru mono ha arimasen)
yYuuhizuNothing can be gained without action.v

(Datte, erubeki mono ga nani ka mo wakarazu, itazura ni kobushi wo furimawashita tokoro de, nozomu mono wo eru koto ga kesshite kanawanai desho)
yYuuhizuHowever, without knowing that which is to be achieved, brandishing arms recklessly against one's comrades will surely never bring about what you desire.v

(Tou-san......ikiteru yo ne...?)
yMikotozuDad......you're still alive, right......?v

(Wakamono tachi no isagiyoki kokorozashi wo ishizue ni, watakushitachi wa ichigan to nari, shouri to heiwa wo kachitoru tame, tomo ni kunan wo norikoete mairimashou)
yYuuhizuLet us move past these trials together, and with the noble wills of those young men and women as our cornerstones, we will come together as one to achieve victory and peace.v

(Nippon kokumin no mina-sama......)
yYuuhizuTo all people of Japan......v

(Tami to kuni no tame, sono mi wo sasageta mono tachi wo, soshite onore no sekimu ni junjita mono tachi no kokoro wo, douka wasurenaide kudasai)
yYuuhizuI ask that you never forget those who gave their lives for the people in this country, the hearts of those who sacrificed themselves for their duty.v

(Amata no eirei no ishi wo seoi, watakushi wa ayumi tsuzukemasu)
yYuuhizuI shall press onward, with the dying wills of countless warriors weighing on my shoulders.v 

(Douka mina-sama no chikara wo, ima shirabaku o-kashi kudasai)
yYuuhizuI ask that you all lend me your strength for a short while longer.v

(Onaji ayamachi wo kurikaesanu you, ono ono ga nasubeki wo naseru you, tomo ni mirai wo misue, ayunde mairimashou)
yYuuhizuLet us look to the future and press onward together, so that we may not repeat the same mistakes, but do what each and every one of us is meant to do.v
