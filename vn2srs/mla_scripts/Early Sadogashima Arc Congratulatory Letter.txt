Early Sadogashima Arc Congratulatory Letter

This text file automatically appears when you reach the graduation ceremony early in the Sadogashima Arc where the letter is read aloud. Simply read the following lines as you would the rest of MLA, using the romaji over each one to ensure you're at the right place.

(Kotabi no hataraki, makoto ni taigi de arimashita)
yGen. RadhabinodzuYour recent labors have been truly arduous.v

(Watashi no mayoi wo tadasou to suru, wakamono-tachi no tsuyoki ishi wo, kono mi wo motte shiru koto ga deki, ureshiku omoimasu)
yGen. RadhabinodzuI was glad for the opportunity to know firsthand the strong wills of those young men and women who sought to correct my mistakes.v

(Shikashi, ikutsu mono inochi ga chiri, nido to kaeranu mono to natta koto wa kanashiku, waga mi wo sakareru you na omoi desu)
yYuuhizuHowever, many lives were lost, and can never be replaced. I feel as though the sadness will tear me apart.v

(Negawaku ba, ten ni mesareshi karera no mitama ga, yasuraka naran koto wo)
yYuuhizuI pray that the souls of those who left us are at peace.v

(Senjin-tachi wa kono kuni to tami wo aishi ikutsushimi, sore ga nagaraeru koto wo negattekimashita)
yYuuhizuThose warriors loved and adored this country and its people, and wished for them to last forever.v

(Sono senjin no omoi wa, kono chi ni kurasu subete no hito ni takusareteiru no desu)
yYuuhizuThat feeling of theirs has been entrusted to all the people who now live in this land.v

(Sono omoi wo hatasu koto ga, ima no yo ni oite naminami naranu koto de wa gozaimashou)
yYuuhizuIt is a rare thing for their wish to be granted in the world as it is now.v

(Shikashi hitori hitori ga nasubeki koto wo nashi, soukoku wo norikoe, chikara wo awaseru toki)
yYuuhizuHowever, when each and every one of us does what we are meant to do, overcoming petty differences, and combining our strengths......v

(Hatasazaru mono nado ari wa shinai deshou)
yYuuhizuThere will be nothing we cannot accomplish.v

(Mina-sama ga tadashiki michi wo ayumaren koto wo, setsu ni negaimasu)
yYuuhizuI earnestly wish that you all manage to walk down the right path.v

(Soshite waga kokoro wa, ika naru toki mo sonata-tachi to tomo ni arimasu)
yYuuhizuAnd my heart will forever be with you all.v
